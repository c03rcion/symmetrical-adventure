About

Completing an accredited laboratory is a very time consuming endeavor requiring industry knowledge and experience that can take from 12 to over 18 months to implement. Additionally, since there is no standard methodology, everything must be created and verified from scratch and mistakes can increase that timeline considerably. These factors can add up to well over $2.5 million dollars in expenses with a potential loss in time and revenue in establishing your facility as the prime provider of your target market.

At TCL Consulting we use our experience in the cannabis industry to develop your cannabis testing laboratory in a timely manner. We will ensure your facility meets International Organization for Standardization (ISO) 17025, NELAP TNI (National Environmental Laboratory Accrediation Program), and NEFAP (National Environmental Field Activities Program) accrediation standards and field sampling protocols.
